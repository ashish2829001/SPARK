About

What is ITFT ?

It is one of its kind in the Road Traffic Sector.
Intelligent Traffic Forecasting Tool (ITFT) is a very sophisticated and efficient traffic flow forecasting tool which uses combinations of a variety of traditional forecasting techniques under Machine Learning such as ARIMA, SARIMA etc.

Our AIM

ITFT aims at forecasting traffic flow at future time stamps at a given section of a road/highway. It uses a large amount of available past years time series flow data available for that section. The fairly forecasted data is extremely helpful in planning future capacity,comfort and geometric parameters of road, to analyze and eliminate potential environmental threats that may arise due to possible future congestion levels.
