Marketing Scope

Our company works in the implementation of advanced technologies such as ML/AI in the field of Civil Engineering. After doing some research we came to know that for 50% of road projects, the difference between actual & forecasted traffic is more than ±20%; for 25% of road projects, the difference is larger than ±40% (ResearchGate, 2006). The reason behind this scenario is that we’re still stuck with the traditional & old ways of analyzing & forecasting traffic. Some of the unique differentiators of S.P.A.R.K. includes it’s highly intellectual task force, implementation of specific & optimal fitting ML/AI models on the given data using Feature Engineering Concepts, etc.

A recognisable achievement comes from the incident in 2017 where New delhi was placed amongst world's worst cities as per traffic congestion, but with the use of ML/AI, the situation was totally changed. Officials from the Delhi Traffic Police were determined to find a solution to their ever-increasing traffic problem & decided to implement a new AI-driven traffic management system (ITMS). After that Delhi’s ITMS model reduced manual intervention in traffic control to a minimum. With traffic AI monitoring the flow in congested roads, traffic policemen & government officials can manage & direct traffic from a safe distance, this has also reduced the chances of respiratory diseases.

By implementing our solutions to these problems we can develop significantly advanced transportation facilities, integrated and smart traffic controlling modules & we can prevent construction cost overruns & post construction optimizations as well.
